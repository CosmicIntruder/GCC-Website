Overview

The GO-AI GCC team is dedicated to the real-time monitoring of inbound jobs for GO-AI associates, ensuring that all Service Level Agreements (SLAs) are met without exception. Once the weekly plan is prepared and published by the Workforce Management (WFM) team, the GCC team ensures that the assumptions made by WFM are effectively implemented in real-time.

Mission & Responsibilities

Our Mission

Ensure all inbound jobs for any program are delivered within defined SLA timeframes through optimal resource utilization while maintaining error-free data capture.
