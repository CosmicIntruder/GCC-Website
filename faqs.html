General Operations

What is the primary role of the GCC team?

▼

The GCC team serves multiple critical functions:

  • Real-time monitoring of inbound jobs
  • Ensuring SLA compliance
  • Managing operational metrics
  • Coordinating with various teams
  • Maintaining data accuracy

The team operates 24/7 to ensure continuous monitoring and support for all GO-AI operations.

How does GCC handle SLA risk situations?

▼

GCC follows a structured approach to handle SLA risks:

  1. Immediate risk assessment
  2. Alert relevant stakeholders
  3. Implement mitigation strategies
  4. Monitor until resolution
  5. Document for future reference

Processes & Procedures

How does the shift adherence system work?

▼

Key Guidelines:

  • Login window: ±5 minutes from scheduled start time
  • Logout window: ±5 minutes from scheduled end time
  • Exceptions require FLM approval

Valid Exception Reasons:

  • IT issues
  • Power outages
  • Transportation delays
  • System outages

How is overtime (OT) managed?

▼

OT Process Flow:

  1. Business need identification
  2. Leadership approval
  3. Posting on Amazon Vibe
  4. Application by eligible AAs
  5. Validation and approval

Invalid OT Conditions:

  • Shift timing conflicts
  • Seven consecutive workdays
  • Eligibility criteria not met

Technical Support

How are SIM tickets handled?

▼

SIM Resolution Process:

  1. Ticket receipt and categorization
  2. Priority assessment
  3. Investigation and validation
  4. Resolution within 72 hours
  5. Documentation and closure

Response Times:

  • High priority: Immediate attention
  • Standard: Within 72 hours
  • Follow-up verification
